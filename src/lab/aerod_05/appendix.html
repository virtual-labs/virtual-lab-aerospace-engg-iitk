Questions

  1. What is the effect of the sharp leading delta wing?

    Ans. Sharp leading edge of delta wing helps in creating vortices of various sizes that can provide suction force for generating lift.It also helps in reducing wave drag for supersonic vehicles.

  2. Does the vortex pair always remain symmetric with increasing angle of attack?

    Ans. With increase of angle of attack,the vortex pair start becoming unsymmetrical as a result of which, differential lift is generated on either side of wing span.Due to this, the wing starts rocking periodically as the vortex-pair try to dominate over each other in magnitude which oscillates in time.

  3. What are the advantages due o the Lee-side vortices of a delta wing?

    Ans. The Lee side vortices of a delta wing are considerable in size that can create large suction force which can generate hgh lift.
