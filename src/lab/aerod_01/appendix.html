Study the Hele-Shaw apparatus





Questions

  1. What is Hele-Shaw analogy?Why is it named so?

    Ans. Hele-Shaw apparatus produces a flow similar to that of potential flow.It is an analogy experiment known as the Hele-Shaw analogy

  2. What is chemical formula of red dye?

    Ans. KMnO4

  3. What is reduced Reynolds number in Hele-Shaw motion?

    Ans. The calculated Reynolds number in Hele-Shaw motion is around 100 which is very low.

  4. Is the flow in the Hele-Shaw apparatus viscous or potential?

    Ans. The flow in the Hele-Shaw apparatus is viscous,it is a surprising property that it reproduces the streamlines of potential flow.

  5. Is the Hele-Shaw apparatus two-dimensional everywhere?

    Ans. Yes,it is two-dimensional everywhere.

  6. What are the limitations of this analogy?

    Ans. The models kept in the slit must not allow any flow to take place between the model side surface and the apparatus wall,this is to insure the flow is strictly two-dimensional. It does not satisfy no-slip boundary condition.
