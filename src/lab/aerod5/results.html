Conclusion:

The ditot pressure profile for Mach 0.3 jet without control shows that at X/D=0.5 the velocity profile is that along the radial direction exhibiting a typical top-hot profile.The extent of radial distance up to which uniform pressure prevails decreases progressively with axial distance.The pressure first increases upto its maximum value which is attained at the center and decreases thereafter.
