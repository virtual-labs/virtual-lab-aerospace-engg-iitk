Studies on Jet Propagation





Questions

  1. What is invariant across an open jet?

    Ans. In an open jet,pressure throughout the jet is equal to static pressure of the surrounding,so that the jet momentum is invariant across the jet.

  2. What do you understand by simple and non-simple region?

    Ans. A simple wave is a straight Mach line with constant flow conditions.A region of supersonic expansion or compression with simple waves or straight Mach lines is called a Simple region.Whereas the region in which the straight Mach lines become curved aftr intersection with other Mach lines is called a Non-simple region.

  3. Give two practical applications for jet mixing.

    Ans. Two practical application of jet mixing.

    1. Jet mixer has been used to mix Tetra Ethyl Lead into petrol in large underground tanks.
    2. It has been used to insert oxygen pores into ice-cream which increases its taste.

  4. The jet gas you studied is barotropic or nonbarotropic?Explain

    Ans.A barotropic fluid is one in which the density at a point is only the function of pressure and stagnation pressure aren't equal,hence the jet doesn't follow the isentropic process and we can't say that the density at a point is only a function of the pressure. Hence the fluid is a non barotropic fluid.
