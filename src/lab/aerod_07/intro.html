Studies on Jet Propagation




INTRODUCTION:

A nozzle with exit diameter of 12 mm was attached to a stagnation chamber whose pressure(in inches of Hg) was kept such that a desired Mach number could be obtained at the nozzle exit,assuming the flow to behave isentropically.The stagnation chamber and a pitot probe were attached to a pressure transducer whose data was aquired and displayed on LABVIEW.The pitot probe was attached to aa traverse which had three degrees of translational freedom. With a fixed Mach number and stagnation pressure,the position of the pitot probe was varied along the centerline applying 1mm of increments so that pitot pressure was measured between x/d=0 and x/d=30.
