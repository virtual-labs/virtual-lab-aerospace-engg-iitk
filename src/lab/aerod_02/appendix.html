  2. What are the differences between flow over streamlined and bluff bodies?

    Ans.

    1. In streamlined body,skin function is dominant part but in bluff body,formed drag is dominant part.
    2. In streamlined body,smaller wake but in bluff body larger wake.
    3. In streamlined body,separation delayed to higher Re than that of bluffbody.
    4. Streamlined mostly laminar flow for higher Re but in bluff body,transition is reached earlier.

  3. Can you point out the differences between potential and real flows?

    Ans.

    Potential Flow Real Flow
    1. Inviscid 1. Viscous
    2. No separation 2. With separation
    3. Body contour is followed by streamlines 3. Flow is not able to follow the contour always
    4. Boundary layer is absent 4. Boundary layer is present
